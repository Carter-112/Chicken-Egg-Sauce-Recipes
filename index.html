Shared Base Ingredients

  • 24 oz Chicken Breast (cut into chunks)
  • 2 Raw Eggs
  • 1 cup Mixed Greens Pack
  • 1 cup Fresh Spinach
  • ¾ cup Cooked Lentils
  • 1 tablespoon Pumpkin Seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Chia Seeds
  • 1½ cups Fat-Free Mozzarella

Base Calories: 1246 total / 311.5 per serving

Original Creamy Sauce

  • 1 cup Plain Nonfat Greek Yogurt
  • 2 oz Reduced-Fat Cream Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Ranch Seasoning
  • 1 cup Chicken Bone Broth

Total Calories: 1446 / Per Serving: 361.5

Low-Calorie Marinara Sauce

  • 1 cup Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • ½ cup Cottage Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning
  • ½ cup Chicken Bone Broth

Total Calories: 1373.5 / Per Serving: 343.375

Low-Calorie Alfredo Sauce

  • ½ cup Cottage Cheese
  • ½ cup Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • 2 tablespoons Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 oz Reduced-Fat Cream Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic Powder

Total Calories: 1465 / Per Serving: 366.25

Instructions

  1. Combine the shared base ingredients and the sauce ingredients in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into a large nonstick pan.
  3. Cook on low-medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is fully cooked (165°F internal temperature).
  4. Crack the eggs directly into the pan and stir gently to integrate them into the mixture.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella over the top, cover, and cook for 2-3 minutes until the cheese is melted.
  6. Slide the mixture onto a plate or cutting board, slice into 4 portions, and enjoy!
