Shared Base Ingredients

  • 24 oz Chicken Breast (cut into chunks)
  • 2 Raw Eggs
  • 1 cup Mixed Greens Pack (baby lettuces, greens & radicchio)
  • 1 cup Fresh Spinach (optional)
  • ¾ cup Cooked Lentils
  • 1 tablespoon Pumpkin Seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Chia Seeds
  • 1½ cups Fat-Free Mozzarella
  • Garlic Salt (to taste)
  • Onion Powder (to taste)
  • ½ Fresh Lime (optional, for freshness)

Original Creamy Sauce

  • 1 cup Plain Nonfat Greek Yogurt
  • 2 oz Reduced-Fat Cream Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Ranch Seasoning
  • 1 cup Chicken Bone Broth

Low-Calorie Marinara Sauce

  • 1 cup Canned Crushed Tomatoes (no salt added)
  • ½ cup Cottage Cheese (0% fat)
  • 1 tablespoon Italian Seasoning
  • ½ cup Chicken Bone Broth

Low-Calorie Alfredo Sauce

  • ½ cup Cottage Cheese (0% fat)
  • ½ cup Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • 2 tablespoons Grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 oz Reduced-Fat Cream Cheese
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic Powder

Mild Herb Cream Sauce

  • 1 cup Plain Nonfat Greek Yogurt
  • 2 oz Reduced-Fat Cream Cheese
  • ½ cup Chicken Bone Broth
  • 1 tablespoon Fresh Chopped Parsley or Dill

Smoky BBQ Sauce

  • 1 cup Low-Sugar BBQ Sauce
  • ½ cup Cottage Cheese (0% fat)
  • 1 teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • ½ teaspoon Garlic Powder

Cheddar Cheese Sauce

  • 1 cup Cottage Cheese (0% fat)
  • ½ cup Unsweetened Almond Milk
  • ½ cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • ½ teaspoon Garlic Powder

Creamy Basil Sauce

  • 1 cup Plain Nonfat Greek Yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon Fresh Basil, Chopped
  • ½ cup Chicken Bone Broth
  • 1 tablespoon Grated Parmesan Cheese
